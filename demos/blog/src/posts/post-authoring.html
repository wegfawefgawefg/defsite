The fastest way to lose adoption is a syntax that surprises people. We leaned into normal HTML shape so files stay legible in any editor and in raw diffs.

Named slots and props already cover a surprising amount of practical reuse. You can build shells, cards, callouts, and content islands without adding expression syntax.

Authoring heuristics

  • Prefer composition over inline logic.
  • Treat warnings as usability feedback, not just compiler output.
  • Keep demo content rich enough to expose real friction points.
