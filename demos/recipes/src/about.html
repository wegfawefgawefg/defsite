About

Practical Japanese recipes for busy evenings.

AmisRecipes is a modern home-cooking project focused on dishes that feel authentic in flavor but realistic in schedule. We prioritize repeatable techniques, balanced seasoning, and clear prep order.

The site itself is a demo for metadata-driven static discovery: every card in the recipe index is generated from `data-*` fields embedded in each recipe page.

Editorial principles

  • Weeknight viability first
  • Ingredient substitutions where possible
  • Simple plating, strong flavor
  • Cultural respect without stereotype
