About

I write recipes for busy people who still want real dinner.

I am a former line cook turned product designer. Weeknight Recipes started as private notes for friends juggling jobs, family, and limited kitchen time.

The rule is simple: meals should be doable on a Tuesday and still taste like you meant it.

What you will find here

  • Reliable base methods you can repeat without memorizing steps
  • Swap suggestions for dietary needs and pantry constraints
  • Clear prep and storage notes for leftovers
