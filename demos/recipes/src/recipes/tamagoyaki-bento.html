Tamagoyaki for Bento

A layered rolled omelet that tastes good warm or room temperature. Great as a bento anchor.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp dashi
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Neutral oil for pan

Method

  1. Whisk eggs with dashi, sugar, and soy.
  2. Pour a thin layer in oiled pan and roll when set.
  3. Repeat layers, lifting roll each time to add egg beneath.
  4. Rest, then slice into thick pieces.
