Miso Soup with Tofu

Quick, light, and deeply satisfying. A good base for any meal table.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups dashi
  • 2 tbsp white or awase miso
  • 150g silken tofu, cubed
  • 1 tbsp dried wakame
  • 2 scallions, sliced

Method

  1. Bring dashi to a gentle simmer.
  2. Add tofu and wakame; warm for 2 minutes.
  3. Dissolve miso in a ladle of broth, then return to pot.
  4. Do not boil after miso; finish with scallion.
