Bright, fast, and pantry-friendly. The starchy pasta water and olive oil create a light sauce with almost no effort.

Ingredients

  • 250g spaghetti or linguine
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/3 cup toasted breadcrumbs

Method

  • Cook pasta in salted water and reserve 1 cup of the water.
  • Warm olive oil, cook garlic until fragrant but not browned.
  • Toss pasta with lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic oil, and a splash of pasta water.
  • Finish with parsley and toasted breadcrumbs.
    •

    Upgrades

    • Add wilted spinach for greens
    • Top with chili flakes for heat
    • Use capers for a salty pop
