Onigiri Three Ways

Perfect for packed lunches. Keep nori separate until serving for best texture.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked Japanese rice
  • Salted salmon flakes
  • Umeboshi paste
  • Seasoned kombu
  • Nori sheets

Method

  1. Wet and salt hands lightly.
  2. Press rice flat, place filling, close, and shape triangles.
  3. Repeat with three fillings.
  4. Wrap with nori just before eating.
