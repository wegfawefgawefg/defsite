Oyakodon

A weeknight classic: tender chicken, onion, and softly set egg in lightly sweet soy-dashi broth over rice.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken thigh, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup dashi
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp mirin, 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 bowls cooked rice

Method

  1. Simmer onion in dashi, soy, mirin, and sugar for 3 minutes.
  2. Add chicken and cook until just done.
  3. Pour in egg in two waves; cover briefly for soft curds.
  4. Slide over hot rice and finish with scallion.
