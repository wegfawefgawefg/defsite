Tonkatsu with Cabbage

Crunchy panko crust and juicy pork with cool cabbage and sweet-savory tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 pork loin cutlets
  • Salt, pepper, flour, egg, panko
  • Neutral oil for frying
  • Finely shredded cabbage
  • Tonkatsu sauce and lemon wedges

Method

  1. Pound cutlets lightly; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Coat in flour, egg, and panko.
  3. Fry at 170C until golden and cooked through.
  4. Rest briefly, slice, and serve with cabbage and sauce.
