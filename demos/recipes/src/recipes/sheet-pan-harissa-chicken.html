This one-pan dinner balances sweet roasted carrots with spicy harissa chicken and bright lemon at the finish.

Ingredients

  • 6 bone-in chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp harissa paste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 carrots, cut into batons
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 lemon, halved

Method

  • Toss chicken with harissa, salt, and olive oil.
  • Spread carrots and onion on a tray; nestle chicken on top.
  • Roast at 220C for 30 to 35 minutes until deeply browned.
  • Squeeze roasted lemon over everything before serving.
    •

    Serve with

    • Couscous or flatbread
    • Greek yogurt with herbs
    • Simple cucumber salad
