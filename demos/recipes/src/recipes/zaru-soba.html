Zaru Soba

Cold soba is ideal for warm days: clean flavors, fast prep, and no heavy sauces.

Ingredients

  • 200g dried soba
  • 1/2 cup mentsuyu, diluted to taste
  • Scallion, nori strips, grated daikon
  • Wasabi (optional)

Method

  1. Boil soba until just tender.
  2. Rinse thoroughly in cold water and drain well.
  3. Serve on a chilled plate with dipping sauce.
  4. Add toppings to the sauce bowl as desired.
