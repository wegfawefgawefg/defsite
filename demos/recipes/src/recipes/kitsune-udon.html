Kitsune Udon

Chewy udon in clear umami broth with juicy aburaage. Minimal ingredients, maximum comfort.

Ingredients

  • 2 portions fresh/frozen udon
  • 2 cups dashi
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp mirin
  • 2 sheets seasoned aburaage
  • Scallion and shichimi togarashi

Method

  1. Bring dashi, soy, and mirin to a simmer.
  2. Warm aburaage in broth for 2 minutes.
  3. Cook udon per package; rinse briefly if needed.
  4. Assemble bowls and top with scallion.
