Nikujaga

A home-style stew where potatoes absorb sweet soy broth and beef flavor. Better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 300g thin beef slices
  • 4 potatoes, chunked
  • 1 onion, wedged
  • 2 cups dashi
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp mirin, 1 tbsp sugar

Method

  1. Saute beef briefly; remove excess fat.
  2. Add onion and potatoes, then dashi and seasonings.
  3. Simmer uncovered until liquid reduces and potatoes are tender.
  4. Rest 10 minutes before serving for deeper flavor.
