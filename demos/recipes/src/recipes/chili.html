This chili is designed for weeknights: fast saute, bold spices, and enough depth to taste slow-cooked.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cans mixed beans, drained
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin + 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 square dark chocolate

Method

  • Saute onion and garlic until soft.
  • Toast tomato paste and spices for 1 minute.
  • Add beans and tomatoes, then simmer 20 minutes.
  • Stir in chocolate, season with salt and lime.
    •

    Serving ideas

    • Top with avocado and cilantro
    • Serve with warm tortillas
    • Leftovers freeze well for 2 months
