Red lentils cook quickly and break down into a creamy texture with no blender needed. Coconut milk and lime make it bright.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp turmeric + 1 tsp cumin

Method

  • Cook onion in oil until soft, then add ginger and garlic.
  • Add spices and lentils, stir 30 seconds.
  • Pour in broth and simmer 20 minutes.
  • Finish with coconut milk, lime juice, and salt.
    •

    Storage

    Keeps 4 days refrigerated or 2 months frozen. Thin with water when reheating.
