Japanese Curry Rice

Mild, savory, and deeply comforting. This version uses curry roux with a touch of grated apple for sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 400g beef or chicken
  • 1 onion, 2 potatoes, 1 carrot
  • 3 cups water or stock
  • 1/2 box Japanese curry roux
  • 1 tsp grated apple (optional)
  • Steamed rice

Method

  1. Brown meat, then saute vegetables until edges soften.
  2. Add water; simmer until potatoes are tender.
  3. Turn off heat, dissolve curry roux, and return to low heat.
  4. Simmer 5 more minutes and serve over rice.
