Okonomiyaki

A crisp-edged, soft-centered cabbage pancake. Top generously and eat immediately.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 3/4 cup flour, 1 egg, 1/2 cup dashi
  • 2 strips bacon or mushrooms
  • Okonomiyaki sauce, mayo, bonito flakes

Method

  1. Mix flour, egg, dashi, and cabbage into a thick batter.
  2. Pan-fry in oil until deeply golden on both sides.
  3. Add bacon on top during second side cooking.
  4. Finish with sauce, mayo, and bonito.
