These bowls are built for contrast: crisp tofu, tangy cucumber, fluffy rice, and a sharp sesame dressing.

Ingredients

  • 400g firm tofu, pressed
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups shredded greens

Method

  • Toss tofu with soy sauce and cornstarch, then bake at 220C until crisp.
  • Quick-pickle cucumber with vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  • Build bowls with rice, greens, tofu, and cucumber.
  • Finish with sesame dressing and scallions.
    •

    Batch prep tip

    Store tofu and rice separately, then add greens and pickles fresh for best texture.
